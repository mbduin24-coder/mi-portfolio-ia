## Post 1 - Double down on what works

LinkedIn gave you a platform to impact people's lives. Beyond just business.
Double down on what always works:
1. If you're just getting started, never quit. Keep posting relentlessly.
2. If you're not getting leads consistently, get in front of more people.
Via comments, DMs, or live events. Collaborate. Fail. Talk. Sell.
3. Find your tribe in the process. Support others and you'll be supported.
This journey may be lonely. But the top isn't.

## Post 2 - 1 hour LinkedIn system

If I only had 1 hour for LinkedIn every day:
20 minutes: Leave 10 comments in the feed (5 familiar + 5 new)
10 minutes: Browse profile views, DM 2-3 ideal clients
30 minutes: Publish a post, then stay in the comments
1 hour total. No excuses. No matter who you are.
LinkedIn is a must.

## Post 3 - System that works without chasing reach

I stopped caring about low reach on LinkedIn.
Instead, I built a system:
1. Heavy positioning - every profile visit has a clear conversion goal
2. Intentional engagement - finding new people in the feed daily
3. Hand-picked conversations + LinkedIn Premium auto-reply sequence
4. Live sessions - LinkedIn Lives every quarter as a conversion engine
Results: Link Up labeled most profitable community on Whop in November.
Now in 68 countries. Conversion happens even when I'm not online.
