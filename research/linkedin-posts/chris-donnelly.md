## Post 1 - LinkedIn profile as landing page

Your LinkedIn profile can be your landing page.
But only if it has the right structure.
When a client discovers your content, they click to your profile.
You have 60 seconds to make your expertise clear.
Results from optimizing my profile:
- 250,000+ email subscribers
- ~$2M ARR for my AI business
- 6 fully sold out Creator Accelerator cohorts
Key profile elements:
- Profile picture: clear, high-quality, on-brand
- Banner: state your offer and direct people
- Headline: what you do and why they should trust you
- Featured section: highlight your main offer
- Call to action: always direct people somewhere

## Post 2 - From 20k to 1.2M followers

3 years ago I had 20k followers on LinkedIn with almost no leads.
Today I have 1.2 million followers and LinkedIn drives revenue across all my businesses.
The difference: the quality of my profile, not just the content.
8 key changes that turned my account into a lead generation machine:
- Define your ICP for the 5% with your specific problem
- Profile photo that builds trust before they read a word
- Headline formula: I help [audience] achieve [result]
- Bio showing transformation, not just credentials
- Featured section turning visitors into warm leads
- Deplatforming: pull people off LinkedIn to something you control

## Post 3 - SEO vs AEO

SEO has evolved. Optimising for Google is not enough anymore.
AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation) is the new layer.
SEO: Getting your website to rank on Google for organic traffic.
AEO: Structuring content so AI platforms like ChatGPT and Perplexity cite your brand.
The opportunity: far fewer competitors are optimising for AEO yet.
Businesses optimising for both will be visible everywhere.
AEO is not replacing SEO. It's simply a new layer your customers pass through.
